Abifor.
Thermoplastic adhesive solutions

Abifor develops and produces thermoplastic hot melt adhesives. We are specialists in a wide product range of adhesives in the form of powders, webs, nets and films that can be activated thermally.

As an owner-managed company based in Zurich, we market our products worldwide and in numerous industries. Our manufacturing plant located in Southern Germany operates high-tech pelletizing and formulation lines providing exact-tolerance performance components.

Our product range

We offer hot melt adhesives made from all thermoplastic polymers in all feasible media styles.

Powder

We offer customized grain sizes and mixtures. Also equipped with premium flame retardant capabilities and cross-linking agents.

Pellets

We supply high quality pellets compounded strictly according to customer technical specifications. Flame-retarded versions available.

Webs

We spin robust webs directly from target high-grade polymers. Available in various weight-per-square distributions and styles.

Nets

We produce open, thin, structural networks with highly distinct ventilation properties. Highly uniform bond performance.

Films

Easy-to-apply solid hot-melt adhesive films available in varied micron thicknesses to yield consistent surface barriers.
